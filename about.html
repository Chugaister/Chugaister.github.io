Hermes is fully automated arbitrage trading bot. Now it is being developed for Binance Smart Chain (BSC).

What is arbitrage?

Arbitrage - is the practice of taking advantage of a difference in prices in two or more markets. Hermes profits on microdifference of BEP20 (ERC20) token prices on decentralized exchanges such as PancakeSwap and others.

How does bot work?

Hermes uses complicated maths to get amount of tokens to trade. Also bot uses complicated graphs algoritms to find profitable token combinations. Due to transaction estimation and extra checks your assets are in safe and fully secured, because there is no any unprofitab;e transaction.
